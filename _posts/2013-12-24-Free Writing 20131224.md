# Free Writing 20131224

What to write? What to write???

I'm not the gifted writer who can just write things easily out from thin air.

I bought a scooter. I haven't named it yet. It's a 2012 model of Honda Scoopy. I got it for only Php 53.5k at Honda Desmark in Bagong Ilog, Pasig. It's actually a bargain because the regular price of it is Php 69k.

I've ridden it for 3 days now. First was when I bought it. Second was yesterday, as my first drive to work. Today is the third. Amazingly, I was a lot more confident in riding it than yesterday. The fear of using it for tomorrow's Christmas surprise delivery is no longer there. I can do it. Although I must admit that I'm still worried about EDSA.

Normally, riding a scooter is actually pretty safe with all the safety gears in combination with defensive driving. The problem with Metro Manila is the traffic and the uneducated drivers. You will encounter vehicles that would just wheez by you without any warning. Some primitive bikers would try to provoke you to race them by throttling loud. There are even cars / vans out there who would deliberately cause you to slip by driving too close. That is why you should never lose your cool, focus, and try not to be stupid.
