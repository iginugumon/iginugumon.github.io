## The First Love

I thought she was that girl I had a crush on from first grade until the third grade. Then she became the funny girl with a pretty sister during my fourth grade until fifth grade. She's also that girl I always wait for in the morning, at school. She had the school girl bangs. She was not one of them, and she was all of them.

## The One That Could Have Been

She could be the girlfriend who chose to go back to her ex because they have a child. Or she was far away while I was wooing her, but she had to choose the nearer guy. The phrase, "You were winning at first..." is a memory that is so vivid yet so strange to me now. And she could also be the girl who had a thing for me but she never had the courage to make the jump.
## The Bad Girl

They were two. The first one broke my heart but my stupid self kept on begging for her love. It was one of my great dramas. I got over her eventually. The other girl was the one who taught me things. Letting go was harder since she threatened to cut herself everytime. I got out with a hardened heart.
## The One That Got Away

She was my girl bestfriend's friend. There was a time when I was infatuated by her. In my consciousness, she's perfect. At times I don't think about her; life must go on. But whenever she comes up to a conversation or a thought of her comes to mind I'm always like, "Oh, her... \*smiles\*"

## The One
I used to believe in her. Now, she's just a mere fabrication of pop culture. Much like Santa Claus. Much like Peter Pan. If I do find her, I will be truly grateful. But if not, I believe I would still be lucky to have found someone to share my life with. And that someone would be loved dearly.
