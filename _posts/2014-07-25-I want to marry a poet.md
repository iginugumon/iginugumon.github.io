# I want to marry a poet

She will bind me with words beyond my vocabulary.
Enthrall.
Enchant.

Her eyes will captivate me as she speaks
Phrases of love,
Friendship,
Family,
Life

Rhymes will carry me
Across the world
Deeper into the abyss
Higher above the heaven
And then back to her arms

I will kiss her lips, which is her art
She will push me away
But sometimes she won't
and she'll kiss me back

Who'll let me know of love
Love that I've already known
Love that I deserve
Love that I don't deserve
Love that don't exist
Love that is eternal

I want to marry a poet
I'm hoping she'll say, "Yes"
