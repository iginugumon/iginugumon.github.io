# Scooter Wants

I am a man of simple needs. I just want the following for my scooter:

1\. Bao helmet
You know the type of helmet that covers only the upper part of your head? That one. It looks good on a scooter and I also kind of want to feel the wind blowing on my face while I'm riding.

2\. Goggles
Since my first ride on my scooter, I find it hard to keep the cover of my helmet open. This is due to the fact that dusts get into my eyes easily. I tried wearing shades but they just don't work. And if I were to use a more open helmet, I would have to deal with that problem. Then I saw a helmet online that has goggles. I was like, "That's what I need!"

3\. Water-resistant, air-conditioned jacket
The danger while travelling is one of the problems when riding a motorcycle. Even if you're the careful type, it is inevitable that you will be encountering reckless drivers in our country. But the thing that I hate the most is the weather. It's either hot, because we're in the tropics, or raining. I know this is probably not gonna happen, but I still wish I could have one.

4\. Closed hook
My scooter already has a hook wherein you can hang your stuff. But a rough ride could equate to losing the things hanging on it. So it's better if this one's closed.

5\. GPS with traffic monitoring
I'm the type who is very uncomfortable asking other people for directions. And riding a scooter also presents the risks of being robbed during stop. A guide would be nice. Just add the traffic monitoring app so I can also change my route when I see that the current one is full-on red.

6\. Bigger capacity tank
On full tank, my scooter can only hold as much as 3.5 liters of gasoline. During my long rides, I had to stop by gas stations every now and then. I have no problem with that. But there are moments that I worry if I'll ever get to reach the next station with my remaining fuel.

7\. Canons
I'm pretty sure that I'm not the only one who had this dream of blowing things up while driving. I may not be using this in reality, even if possible, but I can imagine myself going road rage a lot of times.
