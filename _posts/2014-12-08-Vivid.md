# Vivid

I had a dream last night. You were in it. I can't recall much of it, though.

What I can remember is that it was closer to reality than most dreams. There were no flying animals. Or even people I know suddenly appearing from out of nowhere, while I go on with my life as if everything's normal.

And, yes, there was you. We had a fight. Not a big one. Maybe we argued. Or maybe you did or said something that made me upset. I can still remember me sulking. I can feel myself getting mildly mad at something.

Then I woke up.
