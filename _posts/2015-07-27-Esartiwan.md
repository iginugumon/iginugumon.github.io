# Esartiwan

My friend, the event organizer, have been bugging me since last year to join their romantic-themed trip. I've been quite skeptical about it since it may rub off the wrong way to other people and I was also afraid that the girls who'll be joining are the desperate and ugly bunch. But I also think it's not really a bad idea.

The event was postponed for a few times but it finally pushed through a couple of weeks ago. The name of the trip was changed to a lighter word, though. And it was a good move since a lot were pretty defensive with the word "romantic" as Valentine's Day is just around the corner.

I came in late. In my defense, I was with a girl. So, fuck that 7PM sharp. Good thing that another participant came in later than me so I never had that guilty feeling.

The original plan was a 1:1 ratio of girls and boys. But it must have been a decision with a maximum profit in mind that the end result was a ratio of 5:13, with the girls overpowering the boys. Very much like the real world scenario. Haha!

I've been to several trips from the same organizers before. And here are my thoughts on this one.

Pros

1\. The general idea / theme is great. I've had several ideas of my own on how it might go.

2\. The destination is not the mainstream places you'd usually see. So you'll be spending your time away from the general population.

3\. A great way to meet new friends, the same with previous trips I've participated in.

Cons

1\. In a way, this trip has been lacking on activities. Mostly because of the smaller time frame.

2\. Expectations were high as the photos and videos posted on the group sets the bar up there. And I've been to probably the 2nd best trip they've ever came up with. And this one failed to deliver.

3\. This is the first ever trip with that theme and they don't know what the participants expects and what it should deliver.

4\. Most participants are from the upper society. A lot of them don't want to get their hands dirty. Only a few dipped in the water. And there are those who doesn't know how to mingle with "commoners" even though you can kind of sense that they're trying. While there's a couple who doesn't even want to.

5\. This trip is more expensive than the previous ones even though it offers way less.

At the end of the trip, there are lots of disappointments. But we've also developed friendship in such a short time. We were able to hang out a couple of times afterwards. And just like the previous trips, I'm glad I've made new friends.
