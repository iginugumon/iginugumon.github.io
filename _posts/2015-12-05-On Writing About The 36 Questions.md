# On Writing About "The 36 Questions"

A social experiment was done to simulate the process of falling in love. The experiment involves the partners answering a series of questions. All in all, there are 36 questions. You can search "36 questions" on Google if you want to check it out.

I thought I could use some of the questions as article topics. But the dilemma about it is the questions were designed to be answered with a partner. And if I were to create articles from any of it, it's inevitable to think of someone while writing.

And writing with someone in mind means you will be investing time and effort on that person. Which can lead to an even more fiasco.

Dammit.
