# Le Chelle

These past couple of weeks have been a rollercoaster of emotions: high and low, fast and slow. I knew we were bound to experience this later on. But I didn't know it will be this early. And I was not ready.

The past month has been pure bliss. Honeymoon stage, they say. It'll be over in 2 years max, they say. It didn't matter; I was happy.

Then 2 weeks ago came. The bubble was popped. The whole thing was a ruse all along and I was the willing victim.
People were hurt. Modesty aside, I believe I'm the one who got hurt the most. I was not originally a part of this mess. You pulled me into it and then left me fighting your demons, alone. I was doing great on my own. Now I'm also the one who lost a lot more than any of you in this fuck up.

I should have seen it coming. The way things are moving too fast, a big crash is probably just around the first corner.

It was a quick transition from happiness to frustration to anger to sadness to hope. From hope to bliss to hopelessness to misery. From misery to neutrality to anger to hope again. And finally, from hope to acceptance.

Now, it has left me tired and weary. One of my flaws is I can shield everything else from work, but not from my personal life. For the Nth time, I need a break from everything.

But before anything else, let me thank you:

"Thank you for stopping by."
