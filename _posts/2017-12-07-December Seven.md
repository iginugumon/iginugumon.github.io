# December Seven

At a bar called 7th Heaven
One that has become our coven
Countless nights have made me broken
Months and months I've been unshaven

All these years since you were taken
Leaving me nothing other than a token
Still, I punish myself for words unspoken
Are sins really ever forgiven?
