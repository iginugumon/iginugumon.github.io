We keep the details to ourselves as we get older.

I remember writing an article about how detailed we can be at the time of struggle. And how it slowly gets summarized once you have moved on. 

Now, I just want to add a corollary that the details disappear overtime. You get to the point when you just say one sentence:

“Yes, we dated for a time and it didn’t work.”
