# Cradle snatcher 2.0

Dear Lotus,

In a few months I’ll probably laugh this off. The very idea of me liking someone XX years my junior is horrendous. 

I mean, we got a huge gap in a lot of aspects: age, financial, psyche, emotional… Fuck, 90% of the songs I sing are older than you. 

And if we, hypothetically, get together, I bet we wll break up in less than a month.

Those are conservative estimates. 

In fact, this is probably more about being sexually attracted to you. It could have been some other girl. You just happened to be there at the right place and time. 

But there’s too much energy involved that I wanted to burst.

It makes me think about Ethan Hawke’s interview once when he said that the person who loves is never losing. And the act of feeling deeply means you’re fully alive, and heartbreak doesn’t negate the value of loving — it proves it. 

Not that I love you. But you get it, right?

B
